Abstract

**Habba-Wa-Jumla** is a revolutionary platform designed to simplify event management and wholesale procurement. Whether planning an event or sourcing products in bulk, it provides tools for efficient scheduling, cost-effective purchases, and vendor management. Empowering businesses and individuals, Habba-Wa-Jumla makes event planning and wholesale shopping stress-free.

Introduction

Habba-Wa-Jumla bridges the gap between event organizers and wholesale suppliers, offering an integrated solution for both needs. From creating event schedules to finding the best deals on bulk items, it ensures a seamless experience for users while promoting collaboration among vendors, businesses, and individuals.
